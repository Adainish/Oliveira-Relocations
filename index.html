We Provide the following services:

Relocations

We offer a complete moving service, paying attention to every detail to make your experience as smooth as possible. From securely packing your belongings to arranging them at the final destination, we are here to ensure that everything goes seamlessly.

Packing

If requested, we pack and protect all items individually with the utmost care, ensuring they arrive safely at their destination.

Unpacking

Upon arrival at the destination, we proceed with unpacking and arranging the items so you don't have to worry about a thing.

Dismantling

If necessary, for larger furniture pieces, we professionally disassemble them into smaller parts, protecting each and every one with special blankets before wrapping them in film.

Reassembling

Upon reaching the destination, we carry out the assembly of furniture and other items, if necessary.

Storage

We offer secure storage for your belongings, short or long term.

Cleaning

Our cleaning service, either superficial or detailed depending on the agreement with the client, ensures that both houses are left spotless.

Minor Services

We provide minor wall repair services for small holes resulting from the installation of frames, mirrors, among others. Additionally, we also carry out the installation of lamps and other decorative elements in your new home.
